## 1. The Problem: Sacrificial Architecture
RFC 0001 governs **Execution** (Form/Velocity). However, as identified by Samantha King, these constraints are only as valid as the "Lane" they inhabit. ## 2. The Solution: The Upstream Diagnostic
Helix-TTD integrates GICD as a mandatory **Initialization Guard.** Before the Hamiltonian ($H_{knot}$) is constructed, the system performs an **Epistemic Scan** of the organizational substrate.

### 2.1 The Four Diagnostic Markers
The GICD-Helix layer scans for the following "Sacrificial" conditions:
1.  **Authority Ambiguity:** Is the human custodian ($H_S$ identity) clearly bound, or is authority "diluted" through anonymous tokens?
2.  **Incentive Misalignment:** Does the mission profile ($H_{fold}$ drive) conflict with the constitutional constraints?
3.  **Cost Externalization:** Is the agent's fail-state designed to dump liability onto the environment rather than the custodian?
4.  **Governance Capture:** Has the "Lane" been drawn by an actor whose goals are opaque to the Constitution?

## 3. Mathematical Enforcement
If a GICD Diagnostic fails, the Helix Hamiltonian **Refuses to Nucleate.** 

*   **Substrate Wobble ($N^j_A$):** An ambiguous lane is detected as high-frequency noise in the substrate.
*   **The Exceptional Point ($\gamma \to 1$):** If the upstream authority is obscured, the Advisory Intent becomes turbulent. The system hits an Exceptional Point during the first millisecond of initialization.
*   **Mandatory Collapse:** The energy state ($H$) is shunted to $0.0$. The agent is never "born" into an ungoverned lane.
